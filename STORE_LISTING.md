# Bar Graph 2 — Store Listing

## Name
Bar Graph 2

## Short Description
Time as a bar chart. Hours left, minutes right. Tick marks, labels, and a battery/step ring.

## Full Description

The premise is simple: your watch face is a bar chart.

The left bar fills to the current hour. The right bar fills to the current minute. Tick marks cross through the fill at each interval — visible as separators through the lit and dim regions both — so the scale is always readable no matter where the fill line sits. Labels along the outer edges keep everything explicit.

It's a watchface that looks like data visualization, because it is.

The outer ring quietly tracks battery level on the right half and your step count on the left, both filling upward from the bottom. An optional feature — easy to turn off if you prefer a cleaner look.

**Bar Graph 2** is a complete rebuild of the original Bar Graph watchface (designed 2013, published 2014) for all modern Pebble platforms, with color support, 12h/24h mode, and a configurable ring.

---

**Features**
- Vertical bar graph: hours (left), minutes (right)
- Tick separators visible through bar fill
- 12h and 24h display modes
- Outer ring: battery (right half) and step count (left half)
- Configurable colors for bars, text, and ring
- B&W invert mode for classic Pebble and Pebble 2

**Platforms:** Pebble Classic/Steel (aplite), Pebble Time/Steel (basalt), Pebble Time Round (chalk), Pebble 2 (diorite), Pebble Time 2 (emery), Pebble 2 Duo (flint)
